What is Human Trafficking?

Human trafficking is the use of force, fraud, or tricking someone into a person into doing some type of labor or commercial sex act.

Places where human trafficking usually happens is when a big event is in place, for example, one of the biggest events where human traffickers take advantage of is during the "Super bowl".

Signs of Human Trafficking!

  • Living with employer
  • Signs of physical abuse.
  • Submissive or fearful.
  • Having multiple phones or multiple social media accounts
